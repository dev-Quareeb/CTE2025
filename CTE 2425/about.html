    MISSION AND CORE VALUES

    1. To create a platform where engineering students can learn from practicing Engineers from diverse fields of engineering.

    2. To associate the students of engineering with other engineering bodies within and outside the country and in pursuance of this objective, mandate all members of association to be members of National Association of computer engineering sy=tudents.

    3. To create a forum for discussing technological advancement through seminars and workshops.
